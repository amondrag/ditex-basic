FAQ

Have a question that’s not here? Get in touch.

What are your typical lead times?

Samples: ~7 days. Production: ~7–14 days depending on quantities and trims.

What’s your minimum order?

Pilot runs start at ~50 units; we’ll advise the most cost‑effective batch size.

Can you handle embroidery/labels?

Yes—embroidered logos, woven labels, size stickers, and bagging with lot codes.

Do you ship outside Puebla/CDMX?

Yes—we coordinate deliveries across central Mexico and can arrange broader freight.
